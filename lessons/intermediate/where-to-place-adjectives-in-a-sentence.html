Adjectives - Where to place Adjectives?

We use adjectives to describe nouns, which basically are words that directly refer to a person, object, or place.

Adjectives are usually placed right before nouns, just like this:
  • Michael saw some beautiful mountains.
  • The Hunger Games was such an exciting film.

    • However, they may also be placed after a linking verb, like "be", "look" or "feel".
  • This cold weather feels amazing.
  • In my opinion, that painting is ugly.
